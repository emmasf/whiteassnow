The Dwarves are outsiders.

Disney sweetened these characters, giving the Dwarves individual names and personalities. In most other versions, Snow White spends her exile more as an indentured servant than a cherished guest. Cast as seven robbers, gypsies, eunuchs, fairies or other “strange people” as well as dwarves, they often treat Snow White as an outsider herself.
