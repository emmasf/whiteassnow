The Dwarves are outsiders.

The Seven Dwarves don’t live in the society with Snow White and the Queen. They also don’t have the same attraction to Snow White, perhaps due to a different value system. Snow White learns how to be a woman while with the dwarves; she cooks, cleans, and otherwise keeps house.
