The King

is absent.

The male characters in Snow White play small roles. This is mostly a story about women passing through phases of life: a maiden becomes a mother, a child becomes a maiden, and a mother becomes a crone. The King is especially silent. He appears only once in the Grimm story and not at all in the Disney version. You would think he might notice that his wife is psychotic and his daughter is missing.
