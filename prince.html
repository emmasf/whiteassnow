The Prince likes an easy target.

Snow White's resurrection has been repeatedly sanitized. At best the Prince has odd taste; at worst, he's a necrophiliac.

Disney has the young lovers meet and fall in love, but in the Grimm tale the Prince first lays eyes upon Snow White when she's already in her coffin. Even the Grimms added a soft touch: in their earliest manuscript, Snow White is awoken by the touch of her newborn child. They edited this out before going to print, but still.

He finds his dream woman in Snow White: a young, beautiful, upper class, perfectly silent little thing.
