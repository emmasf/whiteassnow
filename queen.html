The Queen is a classic narcissist.

Stepmothers everywhere can blame the Grimms for their bad reputation. Once the brothers realized that the tales were being read to children, they published a radically softened second edition. To this end, they transformed an evil mother into an evil stepmother.

That's right: originally, it was Snow White's own mother who plotted the girl's demise. Perhaps the Queen saw a younger version of herself in her daughter and could not face this evidence of her mortality, or perhaps she simply grew jealous of the common people's love for the girl.

She is the center of her own world, delusional about the importance of her beauty, willing to go to any length to deny her waning youth.
