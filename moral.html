What do we learn? Revenge is sweet? Don't ever grow old? Can we really condemn vanity and worship beauty in the same breath?

In a classic Western fashion, we think of Disney's Snow White and her Prince riding off into the sunset. Snow White will soon become Queen and inherit the kingdom, a role she's given solely because of her own inevitably waning beauty.

The Disney film preserves the moral innocence of the couple, killing off the old Queen with an accidental fall. Most versions end in Snow White exacting a gruesome revenge. In the Grimm tale, the couple invites the old Queen to the wedding, then forces her to dance to her death in burning coal shoes.
