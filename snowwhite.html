Snow White is an empty shell.

Snow White is not alone in this; characters in Fairy Tale are often painted in very general terms. They become containers for our imagination, frameworks to be adapted to the time and place of the telling. This is why these stories work across the globe, and why they can be broken down so easily into archetypes. Where the essence of these stories originated, we cannot know.

She has no characteristics beyond her physicality: her coloration, her beauty, her youth. She has no personality. She makes no choices.
