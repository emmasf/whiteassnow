Snow White is an empty shell.

Snow White is not alone in her emptiness. Characters in fairy tales are often painted in very general terms. They become containers for the imagination, frameworks to be adapted to the time and place of the telling. Audiences with incredibly varied lives can relate to the basic archetypes found in these tales.

She has no characteristics beyond her physicality: her coloration, her beauty, her youth. She has no personality. She makes no choices.
