The end.

Did you give in to the seductive emptiness of fashion, fairy tales and iPads?

This project was created while pursuing a Master of Fine Arts at the Maryland Institute College of Art. I set out to explore new possibilities for narrative in the interactive realm. Interface design is often built on the idea that the design should be invisible, a mean to deliver content rather than an end it itself. The interactivity in this project aims to be as fun as it is functional, an act of uncovering and creating, and a new means of exercising the imagination.
