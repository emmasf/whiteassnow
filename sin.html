We all sin.

Botanists and some Biblical scholars suggest that it was a pomegranate, not an apple, that kicked off the original sin. In keeping with this, versions of the tale like the German Richilda and Morocco's The Woman and the Sun have the Queen offer a pomegranate.

Disney reduced Snow White's temptation to just the apple, but in the Grimm tale and most other versions there are a series of three. In most cases, these objects are symbols of the girl's budding womanhood: a corset, a comb, or a necklace. And what is fruit but a plant's ripe sexual organ?

We can't help it; Snow White bites into the apple as Eve did before her. Temptation is too sweet.
